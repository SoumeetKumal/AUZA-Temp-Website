Common Questions

Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes! We reserve spots every day for urgent needs. However, booking online guarantees your time slot.

How does the membership work?

Vitality is not a concierge service; we bill insurance normally. Our optional "Vitality+" membership ($150/yr) covers extended wellness coaching and 24/7 chat access.

Can I get lab results online?

Absolutely. All results are posted to your encrypted Patient Portal as soon as they are ready, usually within 24 hours.
